Welcome :)

I am a PhD candidate at the Département d’informatique at Université du Québec à Montréal, under the supervision of Dr. Abdoulaye Baniré Diallo, director of the Laboratoire Bioinformatique à l’UQAM, Dr. Adrian Tsang, director of the Centre for Structural and Functional Genomics at Concordia University, and Dr. Marie-Jean Meurs.

I hold a NSERC Alexander Graham Bell Canada Graduate Scholarship, and was awarded the FRQNT Doctoral research scholarship.

My research interests lie in the fields of machine learning, information retrieval and extraction, bioinformatics, big data mining, natural language processing and understanding. During my MSc. I conducted research in supervised learning, text mining and data sampling methods applied to biomedical text classification.

A list of my publications and some of my source code are available.

You can reach me by e-mail, or find me at PK-7615 in the Pavillon President-Kennedy or in GE-S120 at the Genomics building.
