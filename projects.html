Projects

Some of the projects I have worked recently include:

TOUCAN: Supervised learning approach to support discovery of biosynthetic gene clusters in fungi.

bioMine: A open source search engine for biomedical data based on Solr.

eRisk prediction: Approach developed for the CLEF eRisk 2017 shared task. Supervised learning models to detect early signs of mental health issues given user content from social media.

SemLinker: Entity Discovery and Linking (EDL) system developed for the EDL track at TAC-KBP 2014.

mycoSORT: Supervised learning approach to support triage of biomedical literature.
